How We Work

Simple Screen Service follows a clear, professional process on every project so you know exactly what to expect from the first call to the final walkthrough.

Our Process

  • 1. Consultation & Site Visit – We listen to your goals, review your enclosure or patio area, and discuss options that match your home and budget.
  • 2. Clear, Written Estimate – You receive a detailed quote with scope, materials, and timeline so there are no surprises.
  • 3. Professional Installation or Repair – Our technicians arrive on time, protect surrounding areas, and complete the work to a luxury‑level finish.
